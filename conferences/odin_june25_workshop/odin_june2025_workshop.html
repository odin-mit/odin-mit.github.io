Workshop Overview

The June 2025 ODIN workshop, co-organized by the Open Data in Neuroscience Initiative at MIT and DANDI, focuses on tools and workflows for open neuroscience data.

The morning features BrainSTEM, an electronic lab notebook for experimental neuroscience, followed by hands-on tutorials. In the afternoon, the focus shifts to DataJoint for managing data-intensive neuroscience workflows, including publishing pipelines to DANDI and EMBER repositories.

Organizers

The ODIN June 2025 Workshop is organized by ODIN@McGovern (Vincent Prevosto, Nima Dehghani, Satrajit Ghosh).
